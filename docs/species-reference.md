# Species Reference

This document lists playable species and their ethnicities. Units and avatars have both a `race` (species) and an `ethnicity`. Ethnicity confers thematic Qi affinities and suggested stat tendencies.

Note: Exact attribute systems will be finalized during implementation. The "Suggested Stat Modifiers" below are guidance for balance/design and will be represented in seed data for use by the server.

## Species: Faun

Grounded, resilient, and community-minded hybrid people of horn and hoof. They dwell in terraced valleys and mountain forests, balancing strength, patience, and nature-attuned Qi cultivation.

### Ethnicities

- Bison
  - Description: Massive, broad-shouldered Fauns of the high plains; revered as builders and defenders.
  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Earth Qi, endurance, fortification, unyielding body cultivation.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Strength +2, Endurance +2, Willpower +1, Agility -1

- Bongo
  - Description: Forest-dwelling Fauns with spiral horns and striped hides; attuned to music and ritual.
  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Rhythm-based Qi, resonance, spiritual communication, harmony arts.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Charisma +2, Perception +1, Willpower +1

- Eland
  - Description: Graceful plains wanderers, tall and calm; known for their meditative patience.
  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Stillness Qi, stamina, patience, mental focus, serenity in combat.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Endurance +2, Willpower +2

- Gazelle
  - Description: Swift and agile; known for dancing footwork and aerial grace.
  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Wind Qi, speed, evasion, rapid qi cycling.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Agility +2, Speed +2, Endurance -1

- Ibex

  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Stone Qi, traction, vertical movement, mountain pathfinding.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Agility +1, Endurance +2, Perception +1

- Kudu
  - Description: Ritualistic and stately, adorned with ornate horns; often sect administrators or priests.
  - Common Traits / Qi Themes: Order Qi, ceremonial arts, governance, stability, moral resonance.
  - Suggested Stat Modifiers: Willpower +2, Charisma +1, Intellect +1

- Server seed data will include per-ethnicity `qi_themes` and `stat_modifiers` for use during avatar/unit creation.
- Avatars and NPCs should store both `species_id` and `ethnicity_id` (if modeled separately) or an ethnicity code under the species.
- Balance will be iterated during playtesting; modifiers here are a starting point.
