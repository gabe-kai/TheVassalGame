## Overview
This reference consolidates skills implied by buildings, resources, and workflows. Skills define job proficiency, production speed/quality, unlocks, and effectiveness of actions. Mortals gain levels slower and cap; cultivators can progress beyond mortal caps.

Each skill includes: description, primary attributes, related buildings, typical XP sources, and common outputs or effects.

### Derived Stats
Some skills confer derived effects based on attributes. Notation examples:
- build_time_multiplier: {focus: -0.2} means higher focus reduces build time.
- purity_bonus: {focus: 0.2, spirit_power: 0.2} increases refined purity with these stats.
- qi_regen: {spirit_power: 0.2, willpower: 0.2} raises qi regeneration rate.

## Core (General)
- General Labor
  - Description: Unskilled physical work; hauling, basic setup, cleanup.
  - Primary Attributes: body, endurance
  - Related Buildings: All construction sites, storage, logistics.
  - XP: hauling, setup tasks, maintenance assistance.

- Construction
  - Description: Building assembly, framing, erection, finishing; applies global construction speed and cost reductions.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, agility, focus
  - Related Buildings: Sect Hall, Timberworks, Stoneworks, Smithy, Supply Depot.
  - XP: active build time, successful milestones, minimal rework.

- Logistics
  - Description: Transport optimization, queueing, storage management.
  - Primary Attributes: intellect, perception
  - Related Buildings: Supply Depot, Granary & Millhouse, Transit Network.
  - XP: deliveries completed, route efficiency, minimal spoilage.

## Gathering & Extraction
- Logging
  - Description: Felling, trimming, drying timber.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, endurance
  - Related Buildings: Timberworks.
  - XP: timber harvested per day, quality yields.

- Quarrying
  - Description: Extracting stone blocks from pits and quarries.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, endurance
  - Related Buildings: Stoneworks.
  - XP: stone blocks extracted, reduced waste.

- Mining (Ore)
  - Description: Extracting iron and rare ores.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, perception
  - Related Buildings: Ironworks, Spirit Lode.
  - XP: ore extracted, hazard mitigation.

- Water Shaping
  - Description: Drawing, pumping, storing and distributing water.
  - Primary Attributes: vitality, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Waterworks, Condensation Pavilion.
  - XP: volume processed, purity maintained.

- Agriculture
  - Description: Soil prep, sowing, tending, harvesting crops.
  - Primary Attributes: vitality, perception
  - Related Buildings: Farmland.
  - XP: crop yield, pest control.

- Herb Cultivation
  - Description: Cultivating spirit herbs and botanicals.
  - Primary Attributes: perception, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Spirit Garden.
  - XP: herb yield, potency.

- Hunting & Trapping
  - Description: Tracking, trapping, harvesting beasts.
  - Primary Attributes: perception, agility
  - Related Buildings: Adventurer's Guild Hall, Beastbane.
  - XP: successful hunts, minimal injuries.

## Processing & Manufacturing
- Carpentry
  - Description: Lumber processing, joinery, furniture, prefabs.
  - Primary Attributes: agility, intellect
  - Related Buildings: Carpenter's Workshop, Spiritwood Atelier.
  - XP: items crafted, quality rating.

- Masonry
  - Description: Cutting, shaping stone; decorative components.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, focus
  - Related Buildings: Stonecutting Yard, Spirit Masonry Forge.
  - XP: cut stone accuracy, reduced breakage.

- Smithing
  - Description: Forging tools, arms, armors; heat treatment.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Smithy, Ironworks, Alloy Foundry.
  - XP: items completed, durability achieved.

- Alchemy
  - Description: Brewing tonics, elixirs, pills.
  - Primary Attributes: intellect, focus
  - Related Buildings: Alchemists Hall, Crystal Refinery (modifiers), Spiritual Kitchen (additives).
  - XP: batch success rate, potency.

- Apothecary
  - Description: Healing applications and dispensary operations.
  - Primary Attributes: perception, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Spirit Apothecary.
  - XP: treatments completed, recovery outcomes.

- Milling & Storage
  - Description: Grain milling, storage preservation.
  - Primary Attributes: intellect, logistics
  - Related Buildings: Granary & Millhouse.
  - XP: throughput, loss reduction.

- Cooking
  - Description: Meal preparation including spirit-infused cuisine.
  - Primary Attributes: perception, charisma
  - Related Buildings: Spiritual Kitchen, Feast Hall.
  - XP: meal quality, buff uptime.

- Crystal Refining
  - Description: Purification and attunement of mana crystals.
  - Primary Attributes: focus, spirit_power
  - Related Buildings: Crystal Refinery, Qi Condenser Spire.
  - XP: purity gains, attenuation efficiency.

## Arcane & Spiritual
- Inscription (Runes & Formations)
  - Description: Carving patterns, crafting cores, aligning arrays.
  - Primary Attributes: focus, clarity
  - Related Buildings: Rune Formation, Spirit Masonry Forge.
  - XP: success rate, network stability.

- Cultivation (Meditation)
  - Description: Internal qi practice; increases qi capacity/regen and spirit stats.
  - Primary Attributes: willpower, spirit_power
  - Related Buildings: Meditation Hall, Sect Hall.
  - XP: session duration, breakthroughs.

- Resonance Tuning
  - Description: Aligning materials/buildings for qi synergy.
  - Primary Attributes: resonance, perception
  - Related Buildings: Spiritwood Atelier, Luminous/Celestial refineries.
  - XP: synergy bonuses applied.

- Wardcraft
  - Description: Defensive wards, protective matrices.
  - Primary Attributes: clarity, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Ward Bastion, Fortified Wall, Sentinel Tower.
  - XP: ward uptime, defense outcomes.

## Civic & Social
- Administration
  - Description: Governance, scheduling, policy, archives.
  - Primary Attributes: intellect, charisma
  - Related Buildings: Sect Hall, Sect Archive, Diplomatic Embassy.
  - XP: task completion rate, unrest reduction.

- Diplomacy & Trade
  - Description: Negotiation, exchange, market operations.
  - Primary Attributes: charisma, intellect
  - Related Buildings: Crystal Exchange Pavilion, Diplomatic Embassy, Marketplace annexes.
  - XP: trade margins, order throughput.

- Festival Stewardry
  - Description: Events, morale festivals, rites and rites logistics.
  - Primary Attributes: charisma, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Feast Hall, Amphitheater.
  - XP: morale uptime, event success.

## Combat & Defense
- Martial Training
  - Description: Melee/ranged drills, formation practice.
  - Primary Attributes: strength, agility, willpower
  - Related Buildings: Training Grounds, Sentinel Tower.
  - XP: drill performance, mission outcomes.

- Beast Handling
  - Description: Husbandry, taming, deployment of beasts.
  - Primary Attributes: willpower, perception
  - Related Buildings: War Pavilion (training elements), Beastbane (control).
  - XP: control stability, synergy in missions.

- Fortification
  - Description: Building and maintaining defensive structures.
  - Primary Attributes: endurance, strength
  - Related Buildings: Fortified Wall, Ward Bastion.
  - XP: construction, repairs under pressure.
